FAQs

What is Showmax?

Showmax is a streaming service that offers you a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, documentaries, Showmax Originals, kids shows, sport and more. You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want without a single advert. There's always something new to discover and shows and movies are added weekly. There are no contracts and you can cancel at any time. Get Showmax all for one low monthly price.

Showmax offers two plans: a standard Showmax entertainment plan and a Showmax Pro plan. Showmax Pro gives you access to all the entertainment you love plus live sport from SuperSport, news and more.

Which devices can I watch on?

This depends on which plan you choose and what suits you best.

With either the standard Showmax or Showmax Pro plan, you can watch on a number of devices, including smartphones, tablets, computers, smart TVs, and media boxes.

If you choose the more affordable Showmax Mobile plan, you can watch on either your mobile device or tablet. Of course, you can choose to modify your plan at any time. The choice is completely yours.

Please note that the live streaming content available on our Pro plans, which includes sports, is not supported on video game consoles and some older smart TVs.

Do I need Wifi or data?

Yes. You need reliable internet with a minimum speed of 5 to 10Mbps. You can use Wi-Fi and/or data, it’s up to you. You can however, control how much data you use by choosing from 3 streaming qualities. To manage this, simply go to Bandwidth Capping in your Showmax app Settings. You can also save data by downloading shows to watch offline on your mobile or tablet with the Showmax app.

Tip: To save costs, download using free Wi-Fi (for example at coffee shops, malls or salons) but please be responsible and adhere to current social distancing regulations.

What is streaming and downloading?

Streaming means watching videos using the Internet and requires data (Wi-Fi or mobile data). Downloading a show on your mobile or tablet allows you to watch offline. This will save the content to your phone or tablet so you can watch it anywhere, anytime.

Please note that the live streaming content available on our Pro plans, which includes sports, is not supported on video game consoles and some older smart TVs.

What is streaming and downloading?

The Showmax app lets you download shows and movies to watch offline. It is available for Android and Apple phones, tablets, smart TVs, media boxes, Xbox One and PS4. Go to the app store on your device, search 'Showmax', select and download.

To start watching, sign up at www.showmax.com and follow the easy steps. Once you're a subscriber, log in to the Showmax app on your device and hit play.
